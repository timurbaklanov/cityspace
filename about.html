About Us

Bringing the world together through live experiences

City Space is a Canadian platform for live experiences that allows anyone to create, share, find and attend events that fuel their passions and enrich their lives. From music festivals, marathons, conferences, community rallies, and fundraisers, to gaming competitions and air guitar contests. Our mission is to bring the world together through live experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you need assistance honing your resume or want to know what to expect during the interview process, we can help. Read through the following answers on popular topics to set yourself up for success.

When you create your profile on the CitySpace Careers site you’ll be asked to share your career history by uploading an existing resume or importing your information from LinkedIn.

Our recruiters review job applicants for each opening and reach out to those who appear to be a strong match with the position. But even if you're not contacted for the job you applied for, your information will go into our recruiter's shared candidate database, which means you may be contacted for other opportunities within CitySpace at a later date.

Help us get to know you by explaining how your prior experience and successes have prepared you for the role you’re pursuing at CitySpace. Review the job description and do a bit of research on the product, team, and our company. Come with a few examples in mind that demonstrate your strengths, any questions you have about the opportunity, and a healthy dose of curiosity. It’s always a good idea to get plenty of sleep and eat a good breakfast, too.

Search and apply for a job on our Careers website. Once you’ve created a profile you’ll be able to quickly apply for additional openings, set-up job alerts, and view the status of your application(s).
